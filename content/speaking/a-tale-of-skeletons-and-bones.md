Kickstarting a new app is something really rare in product companies, but in agencies like Synesthesia we get the chance to start with brand new vanilla projects many times per year.

In this talk I cover common issues that may happen when you develop different projects at the same time by keeping the same architecture and approach (the skeleton), and how we came up with our own open-source tool that boosts productivity by leveraging scaffolding of bones (the actual project-specific code).
